A smarter inbox

Ninja IntelMail is a macOS email app that brings Gmail and other accounts together in a single window. Using on-device analysis powered by Apple Intelligence, it summarizes and organizes large volumes of mail and helps you draft replies quickly.

macOS 26 or later

Features

Multiple accounts

Manage Gmail (OAuth) alongside IMAP / SMTP and POP3 accounts, all in one place.

AI summaries

Apple Intelligence analyzes message content on your device to summarize and help you prioritize.

AI reply assist

Generate reply drafts based on the message context, then review and edit before sending.

Events & deadlines

Add events and deadlines detected in your mail to macOS Calendar, with your confirmation.

Contacts sync

Sync recipients with macOS Contacts to make addressing messages faster.

Compose & attach

Compose and reply with To / Cc / Bcc and file attachments, just like a regular mail client.

Built with privacy in mind

Message analysis and reply drafting are performed on your device by Apple Intelligence. Your email content and contacts are never sent to the developer's servers.

Mail is sent and received directly between your Mac and your own email services, such as Gmail. Account credentials are stored securely in the macOS Keychain. See the Privacy Policy for details.
