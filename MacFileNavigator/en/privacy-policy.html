Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

MacFileNavigator does not collect personal information, usage data, or advertising identifiers. The app does not use advertising or analytics services.

Access to Files and Folders

To display and manage files, the app accesses files and folders on your Mac that you select or authorize. It uses macOS Security-Scoped Bookmarks to retain access permissions.

File names, file contents, and folder structures are not transmitted to the developer's servers. File operations take place on your Mac.

iCloud Drive

With your permission, the app can display and manage files in iCloud Drive. iCloud data storage and synchronization are provided by Apple.

Third-Party Services

The app uses an open-source library to create ZIP archives. This processing does not transmit files or personal information outside your Mac.
